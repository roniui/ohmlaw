Q2: What is the relationship between primary voltage (E₁, V₁) and secondary voltage (E₂, V₂) in a ideal transformer?

  • E₁ ≈ V₁ and E₂ ≈ V₂
  • E₁ > V₁ and E₂ > V₂
  • E₁ < V₁ and E₂ < V₂
  • E₁ = V₂ and E₂ = V₁

Q5: Calculate the voltage regulation in percentage of the transformer if the no load voltage is 240 volt and full load voltage is 220 volt?

  • 7.20%
  • 8.30%
  • 8.71%
  • 9.09%
Show Calculation \[ \text{Voltage Regulation (%)}\]\[ = \frac{V_{\text{no-load}} - V_{\text{full-load}}}{V_{\text{full-load}}} \times 100 \] \[ = \frac{240 - 220}{220} \times 100 = \frac{20}{220} \times 100 = 9.09\% \]
