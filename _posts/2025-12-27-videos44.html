Q1: What is the name of the safety sign?

  • Warning sign
  • Mandatory sign
  • Prohibition sign
  • Information sign

Q2: What is smothering in extinguishing of fire?

  • Adding the fuel element to the fire
  • Removing the fuel element from the fire
  • Using of water to lower the temperature
  • Isolating the fire from the supply of oxygen

Q3: Which is the physical hazard?

  • Smoking
  • Vibration
  • Corrosive
  • Radio active

Q4: Which is the correct sequence of operation to be performed when using the fire extinguisher?

  • Pull, Aim, Squeeze, Sweep
  • Pull, Aim, Sweep, Squeeze
  • Push, Arrange, Squeeze, Sweep
  • Push, Arrange, Sweep, Sequence
